How do I submit my assignments?

To submit assignments, go to your Assignments page, click on the assignment you want to submit, and use the "Submit Assignment" button. You can upload files or type your response directly.

I can't access my courses. What should I do?

First, try refreshing your browser or clearing your cache. If the problem persists, check your internet connection. If you're still having issues, contact our technical support team.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

How can I get extra help with my studies?

You can book one-on-one tutoring sessions, join study groups, access our resource library, or contact your course instructor for additional support.

What are the support hours?

Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Emergency support is available 24/7 for urgent situations.
