How do I submit an assignment?

To submit an assignment, go to your course page, find the assignment section, and click "Submit Assignment". Upload your file and add any required comments before clicking "Submit".

I can't access my online classes. What should I do?

First, check your internet connection. If that's working, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, contact technical support.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

How do I request an extension for an assignment?

Contact your instructor directly through the messaging system or submit a support ticket explaining your circumstances. Extensions are granted on a case-by-case basis.

Where can I find my grades?

Your grades are available in the "Progress" section of your dashboard. You can view individual assignment grades and overall course progress there.
