Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a support ticket?
Click on "Submit Support Ticket" above, fill out the form with your issue details, and our team will respond within 24 hours.
What are your support hours?
Our phone and email support is available Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Live chat and emergency support are available 24/7.
How can I access academic tutoring?
You can book tutoring sessions through the "Book Appointment" option or contact your course coordinator directly.
Is counseling support confidential?
Yes, all counseling and personal support services are completely confidential and follow strict privacy guidelines.
What should I do in an emergency?
For immediate emergencies, call our emergency line at +44 20 7946 0999 or click the Emergency button at the top of this page.
