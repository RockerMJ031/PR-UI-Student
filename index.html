Welcome to Your Student Portal

We're excited to have you as part of the Purple Ruler Academy community! This portal is your central hub for all academic activities, resources, and support services.

Getting Started

Here's what you can do with your student portal:

  • View Your Courses: Access all your enrolled courses, materials, and resources
  • Track Assignments: Stay on top of deadlines and submit your work
  • Monitor Progress: See your academic progress and achievements
  • Manage Schedule: View your timetable and upcoming events
  • Access Support: Get help when you need it from our support team
  • Wellbeing Resources: Take care of your mental and physical health
  • Safety Information: Important safeguarding and safety resources

Important Reminders

  • Check your assignments regularly to stay on track
  • Use the support services available to you
  • Keep your contact information up to date
  • Report any concerns through the appropriate channels
