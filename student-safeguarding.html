Your Safety Matters

What is Safeguarding?

  • Protecting students from harm and abuse
  • Preventing impairment of health and development
  • Ensuring safe and effective care
  • Taking action to enable all students to thrive

Types of Concerns

  • Physical abuse or violence
  • Emotional or psychological abuse
  • Bullying or harassment
  • Neglect or abandonment
  • Online safety issues
  • Substance abuse

Your Rights

  • Right to feel safe and protected
  • Right to confidential support
  • Right to be heard and believed
  • Right to appropriate action
  • Right to ongoing support
