Safety Guidelines

Personal Safety

Never share personal information online. Keep your passwords secure and report any suspicious activity immediately.

Online Communication

Use respectful language in all communications. Report cyberbullying or inappropriate messages to staff.

Reporting Concerns

If you see or experience anything that makes you uncomfortable, report it immediately. No concern is too small.

Supporting Others

Look out for your classmates. If someone seems upset or in trouble, encourage them to seek help or report it yourself.

Digital Privacy

Be careful about what you share online. Think before you post and remember that digital footprints are permanent.
