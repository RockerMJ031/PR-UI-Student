Safeguarding & Protection

Your safety and wellbeing are our top priority

Your Safety Matters

If you are in immediate danger, call 999. All reports are taken seriously and handled confidentially by trained professionals.

Report a Concern

Choose the most appropriate option below. All reports are confidential and will be handled by qualified safeguarding professionals.

Urgent Concern

Immediate safety risk or serious harm to yourself or others

General Concern

Non-urgent safeguarding issues or welfare concerns

Anonymous Report

Report concerns without revealing your identity

What is Safeguarding?

  • Protecting you from abuse, neglect, and harm
  • Ensuring your physical and emotional safety
  • Supporting your wellbeing and development
  • Preventing exploitation and discrimination
  • Promoting your rights and dignity

When to Report

  • Physical, emotional, or sexual abuse
  • Neglect or abandonment
  • Bullying or harassment
  • Discrimination or hate incidents
  • Online safety concerns
  • Substance abuse issues
  • Mental health crises
  • Domestic violence

Your Rights

  • To be safe and protected from harm
  • To be listened to and taken seriously
  • To receive appropriate support
  • To confidentiality and privacy
  • To be involved in decisions about you
  • To access independent advocacy
  • To make complaints if needed
